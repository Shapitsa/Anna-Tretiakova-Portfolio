Who Am I?

I’m a mechanical engineer at Boston University with concentrations in aerospace and manufacturing. I’m passionate about hands-on design, building, and teaching. Through personal projects, coursework, and my work as a makerspace advisor, I’ve developed a strong appreciation for designing with manufacturing in mind and transforming ideas into functional, physical products.

I’m especially interested in manufacturing environments that blend creativity with large-scale production. Beyond industry, I’m committed to expanding access to hands-on engineering education through STEM outreach and mentorship.
